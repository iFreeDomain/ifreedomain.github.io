Domain price chart

This Domain Price Chart is updated daily. It contains the current annual pricing for domain registrations, renewals and transfers in all the Top Level Domains that Freenom operates or manages. The pricing is converted into one currency, as listed above. Pricing of all paid domains are based on cost price, according to the following formula:

Cost price + gateway fee + ICANN fee (if applicable)

This Domain Price Chart is only available in English and should be used as reference only. Actual pricing may vary and can be retrieved on a per domain name basis at My Freenom or with the Freenom API. Special and Premium Domains are excluded from this list. Pricing of Special and Premium domains can be retrieved on a per domain name basis at My Freenom or with the Freenom API.
